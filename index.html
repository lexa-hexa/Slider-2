Red Dead Redemption 2
Arthur Morgan tells the story of his struggle for existence as a loyal gang member alongside leader Dutch Van der Linde. Throughout the game, you hunt, rob, and strengthen your ties with the gang to survive in the harsh lands of the Wild West.
The Last Of Us
Our hero Joel is a single dad living with his daughter. His wife left them right after their daughter Sarah was born. So, it's just the dad and daughter living their lives (and of course Joel's brother Tommy). Unfortunately, this happy family scene won't last long.
Assassins Creed
In 1476, Ezio has turned into a charismatic but ruthless young man. After his father Giovanni and brothers Federico and Petruccio are accused of treason and executed by the corrupt judge Uberto Alberti, Ezio kills Uberto to seek revenge. Ezio, along with his remaining family - his mother Maria and sister Claudia - flee from Florence to their ancestral home in Monteriggioni. There, Ezio's uncle Mario reveals their family's ancient role as members of the Assassin Brotherhood and trains Ezio to become an Assassin.
Resident Evil
In 2004, US government agent Leon S. Kennedy (Paul Mercier) is on a mission to rescue the daughter of the US President, Ashley Graham (Carolyn Lawrence), who has been kidnapped by a mysterious cult. He travels to a rural village in Spain and encounters a group of hostile villagers who serve the cult known as Los Iluminados, promising their lives to the cult in exchange for Ashley. The villagers, once simple farmers, have been infected by a parasitic organism called Las Plagas that controls their minds.
The Witcher
Geralt and the other Witchers have divided tasks to reclaim their stolen mutation secrets and gather information about Salamandra, who played an active role in the attack. They have split up to speed up the investigation. Geralt traveled to Vizima with Triss, where he met his old friends like Dandelion and Zoltan, whom he fought for; however, he did not remember them due to amnesia.
Fallout
The world has advanced a lot in terms of technology, with inventions like laser guns, robot servants, cars powered by atomic energy, and many other things. However, everything seems stuck in the culture of the 1950s. Cars, buildings, clothes, songs, posters, ads, color schemes all reflect the vibe of that era. Even all those super technological gadgets developed seem like they jumped out of Star Trek movies.
